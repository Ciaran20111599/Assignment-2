I am a person who loves movies, action, sci-fi, fantays, classics, I love them all. I have spend a lot of time watching movies with the ones I love as sharing a movie together is a great way to unwind and relax but still spend time with them. If I had to rank a few of my favourites they would have to be:

For first place I couldnt pick just one movie, but a movie series, The Expendables. The expendables series follows a crazy bunch of private mercenaries who are known for picking the most dangerous and suicidal jobs around. They work for anyone from the american government to the russian mob as long as the pay is good enough. These movies are filled with amazing action scenes, one liners and comedic dirty wit, everything a movie series needs.
Coming in 2nd place I'm putting Marvels End Game. How could this not make the list, the culmination of years worth of marvel movies, storytelling, theories and anticipation. This movie was the peak of Marvel cinematic universe, and I mean that in a good and bad way cause its been downhill since nearly but we wont get into that here.
In 3rd place for me it is the original predator movie. I love this movie as its a classic old fashioned action movie with great one liners, epic moments, they let go of making everything realistic and lore accurate and just make stuff happen cause its cool. I love modern day storytelling with lore and telling stories between movies but sometimes you just want to hear the classic "GET TO DA CHOPPA"
For 4th place might be one not a lot of people have heard of but a movie I love, Demolition Man. Demolition man is another classic film staring Sylvester Stallone as a cop from 1993 who's your classic wild cop who does whatever it takes to get the job done. But what does he do when he wakes up in the utopian society of tomorrow with the most dangerous criminal from 1993 running amuk? The exact same antics of course!
For 5th place I would have to include "The Good the Bad and the Ugly" as while it is a bit older (Often meaning better) it is a movie I love as I would watch it every Christmas with my Nana because she loves it too and it was our tradition. The dry comedic line delivery, the dramatic sound effects and prop effects of cannos, music and zooming in on the characters, it all comes together to make a great classic of a movie.
